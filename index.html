Decision Framework
How to Decide
Three simple questions to match the right projector to the room, the content, and the investment.
Q1 — Is image quality non-negotiable?
Choose Barco Medea
If the cinema room is the centrepiece of the home and you want true commercial-cinema picture quality with zero compromise, the Medea is the right call. This is the no-compromise choice.
Q2 — Want Barco quality at smart value?
Choose Barco I600
Same brand, same colour science, same reliability — but at a significantly lower investment. Delivers 90%+ of the Medea experience and saves ₹13.5 Lakh on MRP. This is the budget-value choice.
Q3 — Is total investment the primary driver?
Choose Sony VPL XW8100
Sony's flagship consumer laser delivers excellent native 4K HDR projection at the most accessible entry point. Perfect when budget discipline is essential without moving down to mid-tier consumer products.
